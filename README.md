🏗️ BuildMart - Construction Materials E-Commerce Platform
📌 Overview

BuildMart is a web-based e-commerce platform designed to simplify the purchase of construction materials. The platform enables customers, contractors, and builders to browse, compare, and order construction products online, providing a seamless and efficient procurement experience.

The system offers product management, inventory tracking, order processing, and secure user authentication, helping construction businesses streamline their sales operations.

🎯 Objectives
Provide a centralized platform for purchasing construction materials.
Simplify product discovery and ordering.
Enable efficient inventory and order management.
Improve customer experience through a user-friendly interface.
Support secure and scalable online transactions.
